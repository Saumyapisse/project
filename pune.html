About Pune

Pune is a vibrant city located in the Indian state of Maharashtra. It is known for its rich cultural heritage, educational institutions, and booming IT industry. Often referred to as the "Oxford of the East," Pune is a melting pot of cultures and offers a blend of modernity and tradition.

Places to Visit

  • Shaniwar Wada: A historic fortification built in the 18th century, known for its architectural grandeur and historical significance.
  • Aga Khan Palace: A majestic palace built in the 19th century, serving as a memorial to Mahatma Gandhi and his wife Kasturba Gandhi.
  • Sinhagad Fort: A hill fortress located on the outskirts of Pune, offering panoramic views and insights into the region's history.
  • Kelkar Museum: A treasure trove of artifacts and antiques showcasing Indian history, culture, and art.
  • Osho International Meditation Resort: A spiritual retreat offering meditation programs and wellness activities amidst serene surroundings.

Local Cuisine

Pune offers a variety of delicious Maharashtrian and street foods. Don't miss trying out specialties like Misal Pav, Poha, Bhakri, and Puran Poli. The city is also known for its vibrant food culture and bustling street food markets.

Transportation

Pune is well-connected by road, rail, and air. The city has its own airport, Pune Airport, which operates domestic and international flights. Local transportation options include buses, taxis, auto-rickshaws, and app-based cab services.

Weather

Pune enjoys a moderate climate throughout the year. Summers, from March to June, are warm with temperatures ranging from 25°C to 35°C. Monsoon season, from June to September, brings moderate to heavy rainfall, while winters, from November to February, are cool and pleasant.
