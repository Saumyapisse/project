Travel Guide to Mumbai

About Mumbai

Mumbai, formerly known as Bombay, is the capital city of the Indian state of Maharashtra. It is the most populous city in India and the seventh most populous city in the world. Mumbai is known for its bustling streets, diverse culture, historical landmarks, and vibrant nightlife.

Top Attractions

  • Gateway of India
  • Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Terminus
  • Marine Drive
  • Elephanta Caves
  • Haji Ali Dargah
  • Siddhivinayak Temple

Accommodations

There are numerous accommodations options in Mumbai ranging from budget hostels to luxury hotels. Some popular ones include:

  • Taj Mahal Palace
  • Oberoi Mumbai
  • Trident Nariman Point
  • Taj Lands End

Transportation

Mumbai has an extensive transportation network including local trains, buses, taxis, and auto-rickshaws. The local train network is the lifeline of the city, connecting different parts of Mumbai.

Cuisine

Mumbai offers a diverse culinary experience with a wide range of street food, local delicacies, and international cuisines. Don't miss trying Vada Pav, Pav Bhaji, Pani Puri, and Mumbai-style biryani.

Weather

Mumbai has a tropical climate with hot and humid weather throughout the year. The monsoon season, from June to September, brings heavy rainfall to the city.
